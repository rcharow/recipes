Ingredients:
1 lb split peas
4 large carrots, coarsely chopped
1 large onion, diced
4 celery stalks, coarsely chopped
2 tbsp olive oil
8 cups broth
1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
salt
pepper
juic from 1/2 a lemon

Directions:
1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot
2. Add the carrots, onion, and celery and cook until the onion begins to brown
3. Add the split peas and broth
4. Bring to a boil, then lower heat to simmer and cook for an hour, or until peas are soft and broken down
5. Add salt and pepper to taste
6. Stir in dill and cook for 5 minutes
7. Turn off heat and stir in lemon juice
